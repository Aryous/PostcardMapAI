## Design Context

### Users
Dual audience: (1) travelers and art enthusiasts who want to memorialize places they've visited as unique artistic postcards; (2) developers and tech users interested in AI + cartography who might explore or fork the open-source project. Both audiences value quality and creativity over utility-maximalism.

### Brand Personality
Cartographic · Adventurous · Refined

The product sits at the intersection of precision (maps, coordinates, geography) and art (6 distinct painterly styles, AI generation). It should feel like a premium travel publication — think National Geographic meets a fine-art print shop. Not cold tech, not kitschy souvenir shop. Purposeful and confident.

### Aesthetic Direction
**Visual tone**: Editorial cartographic — warm parchment tones, deep forest greens, ochre golds. Inspired by vintage atlas covers and luxury travel magazines.

**Typography**: Playfair Display (display serif, editorial authority) paired with DM Sans (clean, functional body). DM Mono for coordinates and technical labels.

**Color system**:
- Cream: `oklch(97% 0.02 85)` / `#f8f3e8`
- Ink (near-black, warm): `#1e1810`
- Forest green (primary): `#2a4535`
- Ochre gold (accent): `#c4892a`
- Parchment: `#ede7d5`

**References**: National Geographic covers, Monocle magazine, vintage Michelin travel guides.

**Anti-references**: Generic SaaS blue-gradient hero, AI glow effects, cyberpunk dark mode with neon accents as *primary* aesthetic (Cyberpunk is one style among 6, not the brand).

**Theme**: Light mode. The map IS the visual — use it as hero background with warm overlay.

**Hero treatment**: Full-viewport SVG cartographic map as background, dense warm overlay on left for text legibility, fading to transparent so map shows through on right.

### Design Principles
1. **The map is the product** — never just a background element; let it breathe and show through
2. **Parchment over plastic** — warm, tactile textures and tones; avoid cold grays and generic whites
3. **Editorial hierarchy** — strong typographic contrast between display (Playfair) and functional (DM Sans)
4. **One unforgettable moment** — the stacked floating postcards in the hero should make someone want to screenshot it
5. **Bilingual without compromise** — Chinese and English coexist at equal weight, never as afterthought
